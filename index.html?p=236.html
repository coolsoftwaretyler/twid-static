Drugs

This Week in Drugs has compiled all of our “Drug of the Month” and “Drug of the Week” segments into full-length, informational, “Factpods” for your listening and downloading pleasure. Each Factpod contains information about the specific drug listed – overview, science, history, trends, and more!

None of the information in the following audio segments should be considered medical or legal advice, and is offered purely for its information and entertainment value. The audio is released under a Creative Commons attribution license, and you may download and edit as much as you like, as long as This Week in Drugs is credited.

You can stream our Factpods directly from the page below, or click on the download link for an mp3 file of each. Please feel free to contact us about how we can improve this format and make it easier to understand and interact with.
